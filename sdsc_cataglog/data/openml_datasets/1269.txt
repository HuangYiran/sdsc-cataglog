description: The QSAR-TID-10701 dataset is a collection of 4,638 compounds with their corresponding biological activities. The dataset comprises 7 features derived from the compounds' molecular structures, including molecular weight, topological complexity, and shape coefficient. The target variable in this dataset represents the compound's biological activity, specifically its inhibitory effect on the activity of the enzyme JNK3. The dataset is suitable for the development and evaluation of machine learning models to predict the biological activity of compounds and potentially aid in drug discovery. The dataset is publicly available and can be accessed for research purposes.
