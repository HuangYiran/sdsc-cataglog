description: The abalone dataset is a collection of measurements taken from abalone sea snails, including their age, length, diameter, height, weight, and meat weight. It also contains information about whether the abalone has a shell or not. The dataset consists of 4,177 instances with eight attributes. The age of the abalone is determined by counting the number of rings on the shell. It is categorized into three classes - young, middle-aged, and old. The dataset is commonly used for regression and classification tasks, with the age prediction being a popular target variable.
