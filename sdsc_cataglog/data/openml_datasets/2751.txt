name: public_procurement
dataset_id: 42163.0
description: The public_procurement dataset contains information about public procurement contracts awarded by various entities. It includes data from multiple sources, including government bodies and public agencies, and covers a wide range of industries and sectors. The dataset provides details about the contracts, such as the name of the awarded bidder, the contract value, the duration and status of the contract, and the procuring entity. It also includes additional information about the procurement process, such as the tender procedure used and any documentation associated with the contract. The dataset aims to facilitate transparency and accountability in public procurement activities, enabling analysis and evaluation of the awarded contracts.
