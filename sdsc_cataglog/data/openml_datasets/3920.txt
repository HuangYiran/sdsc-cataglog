description: The dataset "Mercedes_Benz_Greener_Manufacturing" contains information related to vehicles produced by Mercedes-Benz. The dataset provides data on various features and characteristics of the vehicles, such as engine, chassis, and gearbox specifications. It also includes data on the time taken to pass different quality control checks during the manufacturing process. The dataset consists of numerous anonymized features labeled as "X0" to "X385" and their corresponding values. Additionally, there are binary features labeled as "y" indicating the final quality verdict for each vehicle. The dataset aims to explore and analyze factors that contribute to greener manufacturing practices in the automotive industry.
