description: The analcatdata_hiroshima dataset consists of data collected through a survey conducted in Hiroshima, Japan. The survey aimed to gather information related to the agricultural practices and demographics of the region. The dataset includes variables such as age, education level, and income of the respondents. It also contains data on famers' land area, crops cultivated, and agricultural inputs used. The dataset provides valuable insights into the characteristics of the agricultural sector in Hiroshima and enables researchers to explore the relationships between different variables. It can be used for various analyses and modeling purposes in agriculture and related fields.
