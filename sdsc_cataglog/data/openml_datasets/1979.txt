description: The QSAR-TID-12694 dataset contains information about the biological activity of compounds tested as inhibitors of a specific target. The dataset includes 8,439 compounds described by 5,346 descriptors and labeled with their corresponding activity values. The target is an enzyme involved in a specific biological pathway. The descriptors cover different chemical and physical properties of the compounds. The dataset aims at predicting the activity of new compounds based on their descriptor values. It provides a valuable resource for developing models and algorithms to determine the potential effectiveness of compounds as inhibitors for the specific target.
