description: The dataset eye_movements contains information about eye movements collected from participants during a study. It includes data such as the position and velocity of eye movements, the duration of fixations, and the direction of saccades. The data was collected using eye tracking technology and is organized into multiple files, each containing data for a different participant. The dataset also includes information about the experimental conditions and stimuli used in the study. The eye_movements dataset provides researchers with valuable information for studying visual attention and oculomotor behavior.
