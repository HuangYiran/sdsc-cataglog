dataset_id: 45088.0
description: The dataset DLBCL provides molecular and clinical data for Diffuse Large B-Cell Lymphoma (DLBCL), a type of aggressive blood cancer. It contains gene expression data from patients' tumor samples, as well as clinical variables like age, sex, and survival outcome. The dataset aims to facilitate research on DLBCL by enabling the identification of potential biomarkers for diagnosis, prognosis, and treatment response prediction. It includes samples from different research studies and platforms, allowing for cross-validation and integration of various data sources. DLBCL dataset serves as a valuable resource for the scientific community working towards improving understanding and management of DLBCL.
