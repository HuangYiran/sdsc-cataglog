description: The dataset DiabeticMellitus contains information about 101766 patients who were treated at a healthcare institution from 1999 to 2008. It includes various attributes such as patient demographics, admission and discharge details, medical diagnoses, medications, and lab test results. The goal of this dataset is to analyze and understand the factors related to readmission of diabetic patients within 30 days, 60 days, or not at all. The data has both numerical and categorical variables, and also includes missing values and outliers. It is often used in machine learning and data analysis to predict readmission rates and improve patient care for individuals with diabetes.
