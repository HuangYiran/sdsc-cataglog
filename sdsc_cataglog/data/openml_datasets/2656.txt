name: isolet
dataset_id: 41966.0
description: The isolet dataset is a collection of audio recordings of spoken English letters. It consists of 1559 instances with 617 dimensions. Each instance represents a sound recording of one of the 26 English letters. The audio recordings were made by different individuals and captured using a 16 kHz sampling rate. The dataset also includes speaker information such as nationality, gender, and age. It is commonly used for speech recognition research to train and evaluate classification models.
