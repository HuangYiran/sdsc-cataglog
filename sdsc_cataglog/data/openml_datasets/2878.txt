name: default-of-credit-card-clients
dataset_id: 42477.0
description: The dataset "default-of-credit-card-clients" contains information on credit card clients in Taiwan, focusing on whether they will default on their next month's payment. The dataset includes various attributes such as gender, education, marital status, age, and payment history for the past six months. There are also features related to the amount of the bill statement and payment, as well as the limit balance of the credit card. The dataset is useful for analyzing credit card default risks and developing predictive models to identify individuals who are likely to default.
