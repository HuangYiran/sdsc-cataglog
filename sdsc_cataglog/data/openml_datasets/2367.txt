name: slashdot
dataset_id: 41472.0
description: The dataset "slashdot" comprises information collected from the technology news website Slashdot between 2002 and 2010. It contains structured data with over 77,000 user accounts and over 500,000 comments posted on articles during that period. The dataset includes attributes such as the user's ID, karma, creation date, and profile information. Additionally, it provides details about the articles commented on, including the article ID, title, and publishing date. The dataset "slashdot" allows researchers and analysts to explore user behavior and engagement patterns on the platform, facilitating studies related to information sharing, online communities, and user interactions in the technology domain.
