description: The dataset JuanFeldmanIris contains information about the iris flower species, including Sepal Length, Sepal Width, Petal Length, Petal Width, and Species. It is named after its creator, Juan Feldman, who presumably collected the data. The dataset consists of 150 observations of the iris flowers, with 50 samples from each of the three species: Setosa, Versicolor, and Virginica. Each sample has four features mentioned above. This dataset is often used as a classic example for classification tasks and for practicing machine learning and data analysis techniques.
