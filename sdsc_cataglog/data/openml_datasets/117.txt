name: wine_quality
dataset_id: 287.0
description: The wine_quality dataset contains information about various attributes of different wines and their quality ratings. It includes 1599 observations with 12 features including fixed acidity, volatile acidity, citric acid, residual sugar, chlorides, free sulfur dioxide, total sulfur dioxide, density, pH, sulphates, alcohol, and quality. The dataset provides an opportunity to explore and analyze the relationships between these attributes and the quality of the wines. It is valuable for researchers, wine enthusiasts, and professionals in the wine industry to gain insights into the factors that contribute to the perceived quality of different wines.
