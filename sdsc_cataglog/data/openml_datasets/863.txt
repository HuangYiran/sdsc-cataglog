name: AP_Colon_Kidney
dataset_id: 1137.0
description: The AP_Colon_Kidney dataset contains gene expression data from colon and kidney tissues. It includes data from patients with different stages of colon and kidney cancer and healthy individuals. The dataset contains 947 patient samples, and for each sample, it provides information on gene expression levels. The expression levels of over 20,000 genes are measured using microarray technology. This dataset is valuable for studying the molecular characteristics of colon and kidney cancer, identifying biomarkers for early detection or prognosis, and developing targeted therapies. The dataset can be accessed for research purposes, contributing to advancements in cancer research and personalized medicine.
