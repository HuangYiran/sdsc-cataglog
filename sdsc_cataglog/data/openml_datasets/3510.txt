description: The U.S.-Pollution-Data dataset contains information about air pollution levels in different states and counties across the United States. The data includes measurements of various pollutants such as sulfur dioxide, nitrogen dioxide, and particulate matter. It also provides information on the sources of pollution, including industrial activities and mobile sources like vehicles. The dataset covers multiple years, allowing for analysis of trends and changes in pollution levels over time. Additionally, it includes data on population and land area for each state and county, which can be used to explore the relationship between pollution and population density.
