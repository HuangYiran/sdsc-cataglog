name: bodyfat
dataset_id: 560.0
description: The dataset bodyfat comprises measurements of body composition from 252 men. The dataset includes 14 attributes, namely, body fat, age, weight, height, adiposity index, neck circumference, chest circumference, abdominal circumference, hip circumference, thigh circumference, knee circumference, ankle circumference, bicep circumference, and forearm circumference. The purpose of this dataset is to study the relationship between body fat and other physical attributes. The variables are measured using several methods like x-ray, underwater weighing, and biceps skinfold thickness. The body fat percentage ranges from 0.70% to 45.10%. This dataset serves as valuable information for studying body composition and related health factors.
