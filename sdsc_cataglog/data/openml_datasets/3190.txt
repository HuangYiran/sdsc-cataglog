description: The dataset ACSPublicCoverage contains information on public health insurance coverage in the United States. It includes data from various sources, such as surveys, administrative records, and public health program data. The dataset provides details on the type of public coverage, including Medicaid, the Children's Health Insurance Program (CHIP), and Medicare. It also offers information about the age group, income level, and geographic location of individuals covered under these programs. The dataset allows researchers and policymakers to analyze the extent and distribution of public health insurance coverage in different populations and regions, aiding in the assessment of healthcare access and disparities in the country.
