description: The iris dataset is a popular dataset in machine learning and statistics. It is often used for classification tasks and is commonly used to introduce beginners to the field. The dataset contains information about iris flowers, with measurements on the length and width of sepals and petals. It consists of 150 samples from three different species of iris flowers, namely setosa, versicolor, and virginica. Each sample has four features: sepal length, sepal width, petal length, and petal width. The purpose of the dataset is to predict the species of an iris flower based on these measurements.
