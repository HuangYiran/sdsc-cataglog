description: The dataset named FARS (Fatality Analysis Reporting System) contains information regarding fatal traffic accidents that occurred in the United States. It includes data such as the date and time of the accident, the location, and the number of vehicles involved. The dataset also provides details about the individuals involved, such as their age, gender, and whether they were the driver, passenger, or pedestrian. Additionally, FARS includes information about the circumstances of the accident, such as the weather conditions, road type, and whether alcohol was a factor. This dataset is valuable for studying and analyzing trends and factors contributing to traffic fatalities in the US.
