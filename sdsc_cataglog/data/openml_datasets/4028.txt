description: The dataset KDDCup09_upselling consists of information gathered from a retail website. It includes both numerical and categorical features to predict whether a customer will be interested in purchasing a new product. The dataset is composed of 500,000 instances, each containing 68 features. Features include customer demographics, website activity, and previous purchase history. The target variable is binary, with 1 indicating that the customer has shown interest in the new product and 0 indicating no interest. The dataset is made available for research purposes to help develop models and algorithms for predicting customer behavior and increasing sales.
