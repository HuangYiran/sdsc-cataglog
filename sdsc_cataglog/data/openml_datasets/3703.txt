description: The dataset "product-relevance" contains information related to the relevance of products in search results. It includes various attributes such as search terms, product titles, descriptions, and attributes, along with corresponding relevance scores given by human assessors. The data aims to help in developing algorithms and models for improving the search relevance of products in an e-commerce setting. The dataset is available in a structured format, allowing researchers to train and evaluate machine learning models for enhancing product search algorithms. It can be utilized to understand and analyze factors affecting product relevance and develop strategies to optimize search results for better user experiences.
