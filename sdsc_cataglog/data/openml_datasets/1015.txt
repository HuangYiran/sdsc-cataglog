name: volcanoes-e2
dataset_id: 1543.0
description: The dataset volcanoes-e2 consists of information about volcanoes from around the world. It contains 10,000 records, each representing a specific volcano and its attributes. The dataset includes various features such as the volcano's name, location, elevation, and type. It also includes additional information like eruption status, last eruption date, and the volcano's population within a radius of 5 km. The dataset provides valuable information for studying and analyzing volcanoes, their geographical distribution, and the potential risks they pose to nearby populations. It is a comprehensive and reliable resource for researchers and policymakers working in fields related to geology, natural disasters, and risk assessment.
