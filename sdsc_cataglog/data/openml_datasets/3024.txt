description: The dataset, KDDCup09-Upselling, is a collection of records pertaining to an upselling campaign in a telecommunications company. The goal of this dataset is to predict customers who are likely to respond positively to the upselling campaign. This dataset aims to help researchers and data scientists explore and develop models to improve upselling strategies by identifying potential target customers using machine learning algorithms.
