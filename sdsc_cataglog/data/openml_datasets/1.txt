name: kr-vs-kp
dataset_id: 3.0
description: The kr-vs-kp dataset comprises chess endgame positions classified into two classes: "win" or "loss" for the side playing white. It consists of 3,196 instances, with 1,566 wins for white and 1,630 wins for black, providing a balanced distribution. Each instance is represented by seven features, including the status of the white king and rook, the status of the black king and pawn, and the opposition status. The dataset was extracted from the KRK chess endgame database. Its purpose is to facilitate the development of machine learning models to predict the outcome of chess endgame positions.
