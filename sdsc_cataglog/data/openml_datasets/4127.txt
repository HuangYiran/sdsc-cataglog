description: The dataset Meta_Album_RSD_Mini comprises information about albums released on Record Store Day (RSD). It includes details like the artist name, album title, release year, and the record label. The dataset also includes genre information, indicating the music style of each album. Furthermore, it provides information about the format of the release, such as vinyl, CD, or digital release. The dataset contains entries from the years 2008 to 2019, encompassing diverse genres like rock, pop, hip-hop, and jazz. It is a concise dataset that provides a valuable resource for studying RSD and the albums released during this event.
