name: Touch2
dataset_id: 42544.0
description: Touch2 dataset is a collection of touch and gesture sequences captured from users' interactions with mobile devices. The dataset contains data from multiple users and consists of various touch events such as taps, swipes, and pinches. It also includes gesture events like dragging, rotating, and zooming. The data is recorded with high precision and provides extensive information about touch locations, event timings, pressure levels, and finger orientations. The dataset aims to facilitate research in human-computer interaction, touch recognition algorithms, and mobile usability evaluation. It can be utilized for tasks like gesture recognition, touch event analysis, and the development of touch-based applications.
