description: The dataset "Snapchat-Google-PlayStore-Reviews" contains more than 16,000 reviews extracted from the Google Play Store about the Snapchat app. The reviews were collected between July 2010 and May 2018 and cover a wide range of topics and sentiments. The dataset includes attributes such as the review text, rating, timestamp, and version of the app being reviewed. It offers researchers the opportunity to analyze user opinions and experiences with Snapchat, allowing for insights into app performance, user satisfaction, and potential areas for improvement. The dataset serves as a valuable resource for sentiment analysis, natural language processing, and other related research fields.
