name: Diabetes-Dataset-2019
dataset_id: 43341.0
description: The Diabetes-Dataset-2019 is a dataset containing various features related to diabetes in patients. It consists of 9 input variables, including age, BMI, and blood pressure, among others. These features are used to predict the presence or absence of diabetes in patients. The dataset contains 768 observations. Each observation provides information about a patient, including their glucose level and skin thickness. The dataset is primarily intended for machine learning and data analytics purposes, aiming to aid in the development of predictive models for diabetes diagnosis and management. The dataset is freely available and can be used for research and educational purposes.
