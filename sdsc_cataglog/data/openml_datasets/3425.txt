description: The Violent-Crime-by-Counties-in-Maryland dataset provides information on violent crime incidents in various counties of Maryland. The data includes details like the type of crime, the year it occurred, and the county in which it took place. The dataset is maintained by the Maryland State Police and covers a wide range of crimes such as murder, rape, robbery, and aggravated assault. This dataset allows researchers, law enforcement agencies, and policymakers to analyze crime patterns, identify problem areas, and formulate appropriate strategies to reduce crime rates in different Maryland counties.
