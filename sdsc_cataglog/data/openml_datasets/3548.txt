name: Historical_Product_Demand
dataset_id: 43624.0
description: The dataset Historical_Product_Demand contains information about the historical demand for various products. It includes data about the demand quantity, the product's SKU (stock-keeping unit) number, the product category, and the date of the demand. There are also additional columns with data related to the product and its demand, such as its current inventory level at the time of the demand and its potential risk level. The dataset aims to provide insights into the historical demand patterns for different products, enabling businesses to make more informed decisions regarding inventory management and product demand forecasting.
