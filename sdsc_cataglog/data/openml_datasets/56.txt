dataset_id: 150.0
description: The covertype dataset contains data on the forest cover type of regions in the Roosevelt National Forest, Colorado. It includes information on 581,012 observations, each representing a 30x30 meter area. The dataset contains 12 input features, including information on elevation, slope, and soil type. The target variable is the cover type, with seven different classes ranging from spruce/fir to cottonwood/willow. The dataset is widely used for classification tasks, particularly in machine learning. It provides valuable insights into the relationship between environmental factors and forest cover types, allowing for better understanding and management of forests.
