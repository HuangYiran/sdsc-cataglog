description: The dataset IEEE80211aa-GATS-NS3.35 is a collection of data related to the performance of the IEEE 802.11aa amendment in the NS3.35 network simulator. The data includes various metrics such as packet loss, throughput, delay, and jitter. The dataset consists of multiple files, each representing a different experimental scenario and containing information about different parameters like the number of stations, the queue size, and the channel conditions. The dataset aims to provide insights into the behavior and performance of the IEEE 802.11aa standard in different network environments and can be used for research and development purposes in the field of wireless networking.
