description: The dataset FOREX_chfjpy-hour-High contains hourly high price data for the currency pair CHF/JPY in the foreign exchange (FOREX) market. It provides information on the highest price reached during each hour of trading between the Swiss franc (CHF) and the Japanese yen (JPY). This dataset can be useful for analyzing price trends, identifying potential trading opportunities, and assessing market volatility for this specific currency pair. It is important to note that this dataset only includes data related to the high prices and does not provide information on the low, opening, or closing prices for the CHF/JPY pair.
