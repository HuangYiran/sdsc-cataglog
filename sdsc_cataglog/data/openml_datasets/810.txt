description: The dataset rsctc2010_4 is a collection of text documents obtained from the Reuters Corpus Volume 1. It consists of 20,000 documents from various news categories, such as corporate/industrial, government/social, and market sectors. The documents are labeled with their respective categories, allowing for classification tasks. Additionally, the dataset contains both training and test sets, with 10,000 documents each. The documents are preprocessed, with stopwords removed and stemming applied. The dataset provides an opportunity for text classification experiments, research, and evaluation of algorithms.
