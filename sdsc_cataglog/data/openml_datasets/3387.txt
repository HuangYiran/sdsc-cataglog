description: The Apple-Historical-Dataset is a dataset containing historical stock market data for the company Apple Inc. The dataset includes various information such as the opening and closing prices, highest and lowest prices, trading volume, and adjusted closing prices of Apple stocks. It covers a time period of several years, starting from 1980 up to the present day. The dataset is widely used for analyzing the performance and trends of Apple's stock over time, as well as for developing predictive models and conducting research in the field of finance. It provides valuable insights into the stock market activity and historical financial performance of Apple Inc.
