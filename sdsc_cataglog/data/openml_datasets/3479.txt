description: The dataset COVID-19-biotech-companies-on-stock-exchange (2020) focuses on biotech companies involved in COVID-19 research that are publicly listed on stock exchanges. It provides information about these companies, including their stock ticker symbol, exchange name, company name, industry sector, and country of origin. The dataset also includes additional financial information such as market capitalization, enterprise value, annual revenue, and net income. With this data, researchers and investors can gain insights into the biotech companies participating in COVID-19 research and their financial performance. The dataset serves as a valuable resource for analyzing the biotech industry's response to the pandemic.
