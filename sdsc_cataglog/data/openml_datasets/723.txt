name: cmc
dataset_id: 983.0
description: The dataset "cmc" is focused on the Contraceptive Method Choice in which women of different backgrounds were interviewed in rural areas. It consists of 1,473 instances with nine attributes including age, education, husband's education, children, religion, etc. The target variable is the use of contraceptives (1 = Yes, 0 = No). The dataset provides valuable information on understanding the factors influencing the choice of contraceptive methods among women. It can be utilized to perform various analyses like classification, clustering, and regression tasks to uncover patterns and insights related to contraceptive decision-making.
