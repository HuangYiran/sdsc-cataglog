description: The dataset pathfinder_5 is a collection of images and corresponding masks. It contains 5,000 images and their respective masks, which were obtained from synthetic data. Each image has a resolution of 256x256 pixels and consists of different shapes such as rectangles, circles, and triangles. The masks are binary images that indicate the presence or absence of each shape in the corresponding image. This dataset aims to assist in object recognition and segmentation tasks, providing a valuable resource for researchers and practitioners in the field of computer vision.
