description: The Credit-Risk-Dataset is a dataset that contains information about credit risk assessments of individuals. It includes various attributes such as age, income, employment status, loan amount, and loan outcome. The dataset is designed for predictive modeling and machine learning tasks related to credit risk assessment. It consists of a total of 10,000 instances and is split into a training set with 7,500 instances and a test set with 2,500 instances. This dataset can be used to develop models that can accurately predict the creditworthiness of individuals based on their attributes, aiding financial institutions in making informed lending decisions.
