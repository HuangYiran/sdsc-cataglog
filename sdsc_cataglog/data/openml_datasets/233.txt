The dataset is a valuable resource for researchers and policymakers interested in understanding the dynamics of the Canadian labor market. The variables allow for conducting analyses on various topics, including wage inequality, employment trends, and regional disparities. The dataset encompasses a wide range of industries, occupations, and regions across Canada, making it a comprehensive and extensive dataset for labor market research.
