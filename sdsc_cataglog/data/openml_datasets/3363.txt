name: Default-of-Credit-Card-Clients-Dataset
dataset_id: 43435.0
description: The Default of Credit Card Clients Dataset contains information about credit card clients in Taiwan and their payment behaviors from April 2005 to September 2005. The dataset encompasses 30,000 samples and 24 attributes, including demographic, credit card account, payment, and billing features. The dataset's primary objective is to predict whether a client will default on their credit card payment in the following month. This dataset has been widely used in research and analysis within the credit card industry to develop models and algorithms for predicting default risk and determining relevant influencing factors.
