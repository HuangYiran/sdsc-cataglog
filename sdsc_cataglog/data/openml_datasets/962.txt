name: ozone-level-8hr
dataset_id: 1487.0
description: The ozone-level-8hr dataset provides information on the concentration of ozone, a harmful air pollutant, over an 8-hour period. It contains data from various monitoring stations across the United States. The dataset includes variables such as date, time, location, and ozone value. The purpose of this dataset is to track and analyze ozone levels to better understand air pollution and its impact on human health and the environment. Researchers can use this dataset to study trends, identify patterns, and develop strategies to mitigate ozone pollution. The dataset is a valuable resource for policymakers, scientists, and advocates working towards cleaner air quality.
