description: The dataset oil_spill contains information about 937 oil spill incidents that have occurred worldwide. The dataset covers a period from 1974 to 2020 and provides various details about each spill, including the location, date, quantity of oil spilled, spill type, and the substance spilled. The dataset contains information from different sources, including news articles, governmental reports, and environmental organizations. It is a comprehensive compilation of oil spills, enabling researchers and analysts to study trends, patterns, and impacts of these incidents on the environment and wildlife. The dataset holds valuable information for designing prevention strategies and improving response techniques.
