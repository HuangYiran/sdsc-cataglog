The dataset aims to explore the relationship between these attributes and the binary variable of diabetes readmission within 30 days. The dataset includes a mix of both categorical and numerical variables, with missing values present. The study focuses on predicting the likelihood of a patient being readmitted for diabetes treatment within 30 days, enabling better healthcare management and patient care planning.
