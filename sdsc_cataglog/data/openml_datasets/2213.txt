name: BNG(anneal)
dataset_id: 40520.0
description: The BNG dataset, also known as anneal, is a collection of data related to a manufacturing process called annealing. The dataset contains various attributes such as temperatures, chemical compositions, and process parameters that can impact the quality of the annealed product. It includes a target attribute indicating the class of the product, either 1 or 2. The dataset consists of 898 instances with a mix of categorical and numerical attributes. The aim of the data collection is to predict the class of the product based on the given attributes.
