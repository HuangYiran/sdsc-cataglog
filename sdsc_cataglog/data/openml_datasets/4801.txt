name: mildew_6
dataset_id: 45159.0
description: The dataset mildew_6 represents mildew occurrence on wheat in the years 1982 and 1983. It consists of six variables, including geographical coordinates, average temperature, and average humidity. The data was collected from 231 different fields across several regions in France. The mildew occurrence was measured by counting the number of infected wheat rows in each field. The dataset contains a total of 462 observations, with equal numbers for each year. This dataset provides valuable information for analyzing the distribution and impact of mildew on wheat crops in France during these years.
