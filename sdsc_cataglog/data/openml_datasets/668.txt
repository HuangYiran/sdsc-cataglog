name: hutsof99_child_witness
dataset_id: 927.0
description: The dataset hutsof99_child_witness contains data related to child witnesses in court cases. It includes variables such as the witness's age, gender, and relationship to the victim or perpetrator. Information about the type of crime, the court appearance, and the outcome of the case is also included. The dataset was collected in the United States between 1998 and 1999. It serves as a resource to study the role of child witnesses in legal proceedings and understand factors that may influence their testimony or the case outcome. The dataset has the potential to contribute to research on child witness protection and courtroom procedures.
