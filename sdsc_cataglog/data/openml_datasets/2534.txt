The dataset provides insights into the fluctuation of the EUR-HKD exchange rate over a specified period. It allows analysis and identification of patterns, trends, and possible correlations with other factors. The data is useful for financial professionals focusing on the EUR-HKD market and those interested in studying currency exchange dynamics.
