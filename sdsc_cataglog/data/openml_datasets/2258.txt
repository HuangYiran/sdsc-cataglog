name: Titanic
dataset_id: 40704.0
description: The Titanic dataset is a collection of information about the passengers aboard the Titanic ship during its fateful voyage in 1912. It contains details such as their names, ages, genders, ticket class, embarked ports, fare paid, survival status, and more. The dataset is widely used for data analysis and machine learning tasks. It has been explored to understand survival patterns, assess factors influencing survival rates, and develop prediction models. The dataset is of significant historical importance, shedding light on the tragic events that unfolded during the voyage and providing insights into the socio-economic aspects of the passengers at that time.
