name: ERA
dataset_id: 1030.0
description: ERA, or the European Reanalysis dataset, is a comprehensive collection of historical weather information covering a vast geographical area from 1979 onwards. It incorporates multiple data sources such as satellite observations, weather station reports, buoys, and weather models. It provides detailed records of various atmospheric variables like temperature, wind speed, humidity, and precipitation on a global scale. ERA data is processed using advanced data assimilation techniques to ensure accuracy and consistency. It is widely used in climate research, weather forecasting, agricultural planning, and environmental studies. ERA offers valuable insights into past climatic patterns and helps in understanding and predicting future climate change scenarios.
