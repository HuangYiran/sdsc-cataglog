name: Temperature-Readings--IOT-Devices
dataset_id: 43351.0
description: The dataset "Temperature-Readings--IOT-Devices" provides information on temperature readings collected from various Internet of Things (IoT) devices. It consists of 9 fields, including the device ID, date, time, and the recorded temperature. The dataset has a total of 365,280 rows, covering a one-year span from 2016 to 2017. The data is recorded at regular intervals, with each device submitting a reading every 10 minutes. The readings were collected from 50 different IoT devices, each with a unique ID. This dataset can be used for analyzing temperature patterns, detecting anomalies, and studying the behavior of IoT devices in temperature monitoring applications.
