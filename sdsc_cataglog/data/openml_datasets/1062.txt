description: The creditcard dataset is a collection of credit card transactions containing a total of 284,807 records. These features include time, amount, and 28 anonymous variables. The dataset is highly imbalanced, with a majority of genuine transactions. This dataset is often used for fraud detection algorithms and machine learning models, as it provides a realistic representation of the challenges faced in detecting fraudulent activities in real-world credit card usage.
