description: The dataset QSAR-TID-10903 contains information about the activity of certain chemical compounds against an important protein target in the human body. The dataset's purpose is to explore the relationship between the molecular descriptors and the inhibitory activity, facilitating the development of predictive models. It has been utilized in the creation of numerous models for quantitative structure-activity relationship (QSAR) predictions, and its availability is important for researchers in the field. This dataset contributes to the advancement of drug discovery and development processes.
