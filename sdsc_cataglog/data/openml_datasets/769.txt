name: LEV
dataset_id: 1029.0
description: The dataset LEV contains information about the energy consumption of households in the Netherlands. It consists of data collected from smart meters installed in around 2000 residences over a period of one year. The dataset includes variables like date, time, energy consumption in kilowatt-hours, and temperature. It aims to provide insights into the energy usage patterns of households and the impact of temperature on energy consumption. The dataset can be used to study energy efficiency, develop models for forecasting energy consumption, and analyze the effectiveness of energy-saving initiatives.
