name: Foreign-Exchange-Rate-(1994---2020)
dataset_id: 43821.0
description: The dataset "Foreign-Exchange-Rate- (1994---2020)" contains information about exchange rates of various currencies against the US dollar from 1994 to 2020. The dataset includes daily exchange rate values for 43 different currencies, covering both developed and emerging economies. It provides valuable insights into the changes and fluctuations in exchange rates over the years, allowing for analysis of trends, patterns, and correlations. With almost three decades of data, this dataset is a comprehensive and extensive resource for researchers, economists, and analysts to understand and study the dynamics of foreign exchange markets.
