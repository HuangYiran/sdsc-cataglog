description: The Fish-market dataset contains information about seven different fish species. It includes features such as weight, length, height, and width. The goal of this dataset is to predict the species based on these measurements. The dataset consists of 159 samples, with each sample representing a fish. The fish species present in the dataset include Bream, Roach, Whitefish, Parkki, Perch, Pike, and Smelt. This dataset can be useful for various analysis, such as species identification and studying the correlation between different measurements.
