description: The German-Credit-Risk dataset is designed to evaluate the creditworthiness of individuals seeking financial assistance in Germany. It contains 1,000 records with 20 different attributes, including demographic information, employment status, credit history, and payment details. The dataset is widely used in the machine learning community to develop credit risk assessment models and decision-making systems. It aims to identify the factors that influence credit risk and to predict whether a loan applicant is likely to default on payments. The dataset provides a valuable resource for training and validating credit risk models, contributing to the development of efficient and accurate credit assessment algorithms.
