description: The dataset hailfinder_6 contains a collection of information related to hailstorms. It includes data about the date and time of each hailstorm, their location, the size of the hailstones, and the damage caused. The dataset also provides information about the severity of the storm, such as wind speed and humidity levels. This dataset aims to assist in analyzing and understanding hailstorms patterns and their impact on different areas. It can be used to study weather patterns, assess risk factors, and develop strategies to mitigate damage caused by hailstorms.
