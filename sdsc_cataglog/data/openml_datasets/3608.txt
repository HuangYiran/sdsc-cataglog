name: WebMD-Drug-Reviews-Dataset
dataset_id: 43683.0
description: The WebMD-Drug-Reviews-Dataset is a collection of 29,387 drug reviews acquired from the website WebMD. The reviews are written by users and cover a wide range of medications, providing valuable insights into actual patient experiences. Each review includes various attributes such as drug name, condition treated, review rating, review title, review text, and more. The dataset offers an opportunity to analyze sentiments, identify patterns, and understand the efficacy of different medications. It can be used for sentiment analysis, natural language processing, and machine learning tasks to improve drug recommendation systems, gain insights into drug effectiveness, and enhance patient care.
