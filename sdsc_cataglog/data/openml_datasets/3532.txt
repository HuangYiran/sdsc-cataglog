description: The dataset "Personality-traits-of-Twitter-users- (celebrities)" contains information about the personality traits of various celebrities on Twitter. The dataset includes celebrities' Twitter handles, the number of followers they have, and their personality scores across different personality traits such as openness, conscientiousness, extraversion, agreeableness, and emotional stability. The purpose of this dataset is to analyze and understand the personality characteristics of celebrities based on their social media presence. This dataset can be used to explore the relationship between personality traits and online behavior, as well as to study the impact of celebrity personalities on their Twitter followers.
