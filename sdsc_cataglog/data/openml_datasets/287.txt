description: The dataset cps_85_wages provides information on wages for workers in the year 1985. It contains data from the Current Population Survey (CPS), which is a monthly survey conducted by the United States Census Bureau. The dataset includes various variables such as demographic information, employment status, education, occupation, and earnings. It is a comprehensive source of data that can be used to analyze and understand wage trends, inequalities, and factors influencing earnings in 1985. Researchers can utilize this dataset to study the wages of different groups in the workforce and explore the impact of various factors on earnings during that time.
