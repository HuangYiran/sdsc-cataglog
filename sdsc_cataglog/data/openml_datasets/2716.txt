Additionally, it provides detailed ratings and reviews by users, including descriptions, scores, and sentiment analysis. The dataset also offers location information, allowing analysis by country, state, and city. With its extensive attributes and user-generated content, the beer_reviews dataset provides an invaluable resource for studying beer preferences, trends, and sentiments on a global scale.
