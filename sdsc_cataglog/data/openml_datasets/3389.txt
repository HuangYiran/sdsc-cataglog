description: The Riga-real-estate-dataset is a dataset that contains information about real estate properties in Riga, Latvia. It includes data from 2016 to 2019 and consists of over 20,000 records. The dataset contains various attributes, such as the location, size, condition, price, and type of property. It also includes additional information like the number of rooms, floor level, and the presence of amenities like parking or balcony. This dataset provides valuable information for analyzing real estate trends and patterns in Riga, allowing researchers, analysts, and businesses to make informed decisions in the real estate market.
