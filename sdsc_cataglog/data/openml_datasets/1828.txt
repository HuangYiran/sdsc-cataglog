dataset_id: 3812.0
description: QSAR-TID-50 is a dataset comprising 1055 chemical compounds. These compounds are classified into two categories: active and inactive, based on their ability to inhibit human breast cancer cell lines. The dataset includes various molecular descriptors, such as topological, constitutional, and electronic properties. Additionally, it contains the ID and classification of each compound. This dataset is widely used in the field of QSAR (Quantitative Structure-Activity Relationship) studies, aiming to establish a relationship between the chemical structure of molecules and their biological activities. The QSAR-TID-50 dataset provides a valuable resource for researchers exploring breast cancer inhibitors and developing new drugs.
