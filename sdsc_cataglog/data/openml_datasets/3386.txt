description: The dataset Metro-Manila-Flood-Landscape-Data focuses on collecting information about flood vulnerability and hazards in the Metro Manila area. It includes data on various factors such as land elevation, river networks, population density, and rainfall patterns. The aim is to provide an in-depth understanding of the landscape's flood susceptibility and help develop effective mitigation and adaptation strategies. The dataset also incorporates information on infrastructure, such as roads and buildings, and their vulnerability to flooding. This comprehensive dataset can be used by researchers, policymakers, and urban planners to make informed decisions for flood management and urban development in the Metro Manila region.
