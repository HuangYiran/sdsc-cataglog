The dataset was curated from various sources and is predominantly used for developing quantitative structure-activity relationship (QSAR) models. The primary objective of this dataset is to assist in the prediction of compound activity towards the target protein based on their chemical features. The dataset is publicly available and can be utilized for research purposes in drug discovery and design.
