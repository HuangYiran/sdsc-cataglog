description: The dataset KNYC-Metars-2016 contains meteorological data for New York City in the year 2016. It consists of information collected from the weather station at Central Park and provides detailed observations on different weather parameters like temperature, humidity, wind speed and direction, precipitation, and atmospheric pressure. The dataset is accessible in multiple formats and can be used for various purposes like analyzing climate trends, studying weather patterns, and evaluating the impact of weather on different sectors such as agriculture, transportation, and urban planning. The availability of this dataset helps researchers, meteorologists, and policymakers make informed decisions based on accurate and comprehensive weather information.
