name: usp05
dataset_id: 1047.0
description: The dataset usp05 contains information about the daily prices of electricity futures contracts from March 2004 to December 2008. It includes data on the trading volume, open interest, and settlement price of the contracts. The dataset has 6 columns: Date, Trading Volume, Open Interest, Settlement Price, Time Stamp, and Hourly Indicator. The Date column captures the date of the observation, while the Time Stamp column records the time of the observation within a day. The Hourly Indicator column differentiates between hourly and daily observations. This dataset provides a comprehensive view of the electricity futures market during the mentioned period.
