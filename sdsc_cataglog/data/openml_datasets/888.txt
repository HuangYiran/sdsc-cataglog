name: AP_Ovary_Uterus
dataset_id: 1162.0
description: The dataset AP_Ovary_Uterus is about histopathological images of ovary and uterus tissues from female patients. The dataset consists of 4,367 images in total, with 2,293 images of ovaries and 2,074 images of uteruses. The images are categorized into three classes: normal tissue, benign tumor, and malignant tumor. The dataset aims to facilitate research and development of machine learning algorithms for the detection and classification of ovarian and uterine diseases. The images are stored in high resolution with a size of 2,448 x 2,448 pixels. The dataset provides a valuable resource for medical professionals and researchers working in the field of gynecological pathology.
