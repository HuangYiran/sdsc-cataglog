name: Wikidata
dataset_id: 4139.0
description: The Wikidata dataset is a collaborative project that aims to collect and organize structured information about various entities such as people, places, and events. It is an open-source and freely accessible knowledge graph that is updated and maintained by a community of contributors from around the world. The dataset contains multiple types of data, including statements, qualifiers, and references. Each entity in the dataset is assigned a unique identifier, which helps in linking related information across different language editions of Wikipedia and other projects. The Wikidata dataset acts as a central hub for information and supports various applications and research projects.
