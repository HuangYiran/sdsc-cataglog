description: The dataset analcatdata_uktrainacc contains information about train accidents in the UK. It includes various attributes such as the severity of the accident, the type of train involved, the number of fatalities and injuries, the cause of the accident, and the date and time of occurrence. The dataset was derived from the UK Accident Data Archive and can be used for analyzing patterns and factors contributing to train accidents. The dataset consists of 2,282 instances and does not contain missing values. It provides a valuable resource for studying and improving safety measures in the UK's train transportation system.
